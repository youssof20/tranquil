Privacy Notice

UnTrap runs locally by default.
No browsing history, usage data, or personal information is collected or transmitted unless you explicitly enable cloud features.

Local Mode (default)

All features and settings work entirely on your device.
No data leaves your computer.

Optional Cloud Features

If you enable any of these:

  • Account / Sync - Stores your email address and extension preferences on our secure server.
  • Video Summarization - Temporarily sends video transcript or comments for AI processing. Summaries are stored anonymously and never linked to your identity.

All transfers are encrypted and occur only after you opt in.

You can always use UnTrap completely offline in Local Mode.
